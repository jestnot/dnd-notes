# Pre-Campaign Set Up
-  [[The Long Sight]] have already stumbled upon [[content/World of Kyrka/Objects and Documents/The Siphon/Treasure Hunt Locations/Key Trial 1]]'s location, and found that the final door cannot be opened without destroying [[Athtoc's Dreq]] in the ritual chamber
	- They heard about crafty bandits who broke into this area to find rumored treasure of great power, but were destroyed by "a demon"
	- They sent members to find this demon and uncover the power, hoping to give it to their leader to wield and reconquer Kyrka
	- The members captured the Dreq, but wanted to find a host that was easier to restrain and return to the trial location
	- One of the members found [[Athtoc Xarcrath]] and attempted to kidnap him
		- It went wrong, and when the members found the kidnapper's dead body, all they could learn was that a young silver dragonborn was the one with the Dreq -- they have been searching for him since
	- There aren't many silver dragonborns, so [[The Long Sight]] have actually targeted [[Clyberius]]'s current boyfriend (also a silver dragonborn)
- [[The Golden Scales]] has found a potential way to destroy [[content/World of Kyrka/Objects and Documents/The Siphon/The Siphon]] -- they believe that they can bring it to the depths of [[Domedag's Abyss]] and destroy it there
	- At the end of [[content/History of Kyrka/Nackanas-Pinntorp War]], [[The Golden Scales]] manipulated the Nackanian and Pinntorper governments to each destroy ancient artifacts in there as part of the peace treaty
## Act 0: Introduction to the world
- Party retrieves something from the highest layer of Domedag's Abyss
	- Inspired by Made in Abyss
	- Use the "Into the Cess & Citadel" book as inspiration
	- Maybe a holiday -- anniversary of Domedag's death
	- The "Disneyland" around the abyss has a major event, and families come to visit; children on rides

- Maybe the party are all passengers on a train -- which is secretly delivering the relics to be thrown into the Abyss
	- The train passengers just think it's a train for "Domedagland" visitors who are going for the "Celebration of Peace" and there are some VIPs on the train who happen to be there
	- Each PC needs a reason they somehow got a fancy ticket on the train (did they buy it? steal it? were they hired by a rich patron who covered it?)
	- Burglars attempt to rob the train (inspired by The Fantasy Forge's train dungeon)
		- PCs chase after the burglars, who sprint towards the front of the train, where the cargo is
		- Burglars attempt to seize the cargo and disconnect the car from the rest of the train (so that they just have the engine and cargo)

## Act 1 Goals

	- Learn the Key Trial locations

	- Desire to race against these organizations to find the keys first

### Set Up
- PCs find their way to Pinntorper city where [[The Museum]] is
	- [[The Museum]] is the oldest museum in Kyrka -- it was far enough from [[Domedag's Abyss]] that it wasn't destroyed
	- Perhaps) unbeknownst to the PCs, [[content/World of Kyrka/Objects and Documents/The Siphon/Treasure Hunt Locations/The Map Room]] is here
	- **Each PC needs a reason for being in the city, and going to the gala**
- In a few days, [[The Museum Gala Event]] will be held here as the 3 comets pass overhead
	- Every 7 years, there is a charity fundraiser to help support [[The Museum]]
	- Travelers (especially the rich who have money to spare and reputations to uphold) travel from across Kyrka (even from [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/The Three Nations/Nation - Nackanas/Nackanas]] and [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/The Three Nations/Nation - Vangsta/Vangsta]]) for this event
		- In keeping with [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/The Three Nations/Nation - Pinntorp/Pinntorp]]'s philosophy about knowledge, [[The Museum]]'s wealth of knowledge is for all Kyrkans -- not just for Pinntorpers
	- [[Museum Curator]] is responsible for planning the event, but is not a major public-facing figure
		- They have set up extra precautions around [[content/World of Kyrka/Objects and Documents/The Siphon/The Siphon Map]], which is removed from its normal display case and kept in an underground vault (with traps and guards)
			- *finally a time to use Symbol?*
	- This gala will unveil a new [[Domedag]] exhibit -- even though it's been decades since 0 AP, researchers are still finding more information from the corpse and abyss
		- The new main display is rumored to be part of Domedag's corpse, which has never rotted
		- Rumors are that a claw or tooth may be shown, which is very rare -- the [[Domedag Researchers]] rarely allow any specimens outside [[Domedag's Abyss City]]
		- Other rumors claim that it may be information on what lies within [[Domedag's Abyss]] -- only a few explorers have returned to the surface, as magical teleportation is blocked
- [[content/World of Kyrka/Objects and Documents/The Siphon/The Siphon Map]] is hidden within a permanent display in [[The Museum]]: the [[Common Rosetta Stone]]

### Encounters
- [[The Museum Gala Event]] begins, and the PCs notice things going wrong
	- One of [[The Long Sight]] has knocked out a guard and assumed their identity
	- A handful of the "guests" are part of [[The Long Sight]] and are gathering around various exhibits to get into position
		- One of them mentions the cue they're waiting for
	- Some of the seals around the taxidermized displays have been altered/destroyed subtly
- There is a major event intended to happen as the 3 comets pass overhead
	- *Maybe there's a planetarium with a domed glass roof set up to light up as the comets converge -- and this happens during cocktail hour*
- [[The Long Sight]] move off a cue of the 3 comets passing overhead and take action
	- They believe that [[content/World of Kyrka/Objects and Documents/The Siphon/The Siphon Map]] is hidden in [[Common Rosetta Stone]], and a few of them dash towards the basement doors
		- The undercover guard lets them through and watches the door -- this is clearly a coordinated effort
	- The undercover guests spring into action and use necromancy to reanimate the taxidermized exhibits and cause chaos for the guards to deal with
		- The rich guests have some personal bodyguards
	- The museum goes into lockdown, and the walls prevent teleportation
- The PCs follow the infiltrators into the basement
	- The infiltrators notably rush past rooms with expensive artifacts (e.g. gold, diamonds) and beeline for where [[Common Rosetta Stone]] is being stored
	- As the PCs and infiltrators arrive, they see [[content/World of Kyrka/Objects and Documents/The Siphon/Treasure Hunt Locations/The Map Room]] glow -- the 3 comets converge and the map is projected
		- One of the infiltrators takes out a prepared parchment and quill and starts copying down the map
		- The other infiltrators attempt to keep the party away from that one, and help them flee
		- The infiltrators will try to take [[Common Rosetta Stone]] with them
