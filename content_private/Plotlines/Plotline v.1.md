# Pre-Campaign Set Up
-  [[The Naturalists]] have already stumbled upon [[Key Trial 1]]'s location, and found that the final door cannot be opened without destroying [[Athtoc's Dreq]] in the ritual chamber
	- They heard about crafty bandits who broke into this area to find rumored treasure of great power, but were destroyed by "a demon"
	- They sent members to find this demon and uncover the power, hoping to give it to their leader to wield and reconquer Kyrka
	- The members captured the Dreq, but wanted to find a host that was easier to restrain and return to the trial location
	- One of the members found [[Athtoc Xarcrath]] and attempted to kidnap him
		- It went wrong, and when the members found the kidnapper's dead body, all they could learn was that a young silver dragonborn was the one with the Dreq -- they have been searching for him since
	- There aren't many silver dragonborns, so [[The Naturalists]] have actually targeted [[Clyberius]]'s current boyfriend (also a silver dragonborn)
# Act 1: Discovery of the Siphon
## Goals
- Learn about [[The Siphon]] (and the map/trials needed to reach it)
	- Learn the Key Trial locations
- Learn about the existence and motivations of [[The Order of the Golden Scales]] and [[The Naturalists
	- Desire to race against these organizations to find the keys first
- Destroy [[Athtoc's Dreq]] and unlock [[Key Trial 1]]
- Learn of [[The Equalists]]' existence
- [[The Naturalists]]' leader starts to propose more radical laws, including a change to Pinntorp's voting system (to shift power to long-lifers)
	- Instead of using a logarithmic calculation for votes, the # of votes will be proportional to how recently a reference was made -- and more references will simply mean more votes
	- This is intended to obfuscate the shift in power so people don't realize it until it's too late
- [[The Naturalists]]' leader goes public with his plan to acquire more Nackanian land (claiming that Pinntorpers can make better use of land with ley line convergences than the antimagic Nackanians)
	- After the recent [[Nackanas-Pinntorp War]], Pinntorp created a hospital where they took land (at the ley line intersection) and has used it to help heal many people
## Arc 1: Night at the Museum
### Goals
- Introduce [[The Naturalists]] as an antagonistic organization
	- Give the PCs motivation to follow-up on what [[The Naturalists]] want with [[The Siphon]]
- Reveal [[The Siphon Map]] -- and that there are 3 keys needed to unlock the vault where it is being held
- Start to give the PCs a name for themselves, as the ones who saved [[The Gala Event]]
- MAYBE
	- Imply that [[Museum Curator]] knows more about [[The Siphon]] than they're letting on
### NPC Activity
- [[The Order of the Golden Scales]]
	- Is searching for [[Athtoc's Dreq]] so they can reset [[Key Trial 1]]
	- Is aware that others may be aware of [[Key Trial 1]], and have tasked [[Museum Curator]] with tightening security around [[The Museum]]
- [[The Naturalists]]
	- Is searching for [[Athtoc's Dreq]] so they can pass [[Key Trial 1]]
	- Is searching for [[The Siphon Map]] so that they can learn the locations of [[Key Trial 2]] and [[Key Trial 3]]
		- Has found the location of where [[The Siphon Map]] is, and learned that it can only be ready when the 3 comets converge overhead
- [[The Equalists]]
	- Has heard of [[The Siphon]] and wants to find it
		- Has heard that there is a map that shows where [[The Siphon]] is, but doesn't know that there are trials set up to get it
	- Wants to figure out how to sway all of Kyrka against magic-users, starting with converting the stubborn Pinntorpers

### Set Up
- PCs find their way to Pinntorper city where [[The Museum]] is
	- [[The Museum]] is the oldest museum in Kyrka -- it was far enough from [[Domedag's Abyss]] that it wasn't destroyed
	- Perhaps) unbeknownst to the PCs, [[The Map Room]] is here
	- **Each PC needs a reason for being in the city, and going to the gala**
- In a few days, [[The Gala Event]] will be held here as the 3 comets pass overhead
	- Every 7 years, there is a charity fundraiser to help support [[The Museum]]
	- Travelers (especially the rich who have money to spare and reputations to uphold) travel from across Kyrka (even from [[Nackanas]] and [[Vangsta]]) for this event
		- In keeping with [[Pinntorp]]'s philosophy about knowledge, [[The Museum]]'s wealth of knowledge is for all Kyrkans -- not just for Pinntorpers
	- [[Museum Curator]] is responsible for planning the event, but is not a major public-facing figure
		- They have set up extra precautions around [[The Siphon Map]], which is removed from its normal display case and kept in an underground vault (with traps and guards)
			- *finally a time to use Symbol?*
	- This gala will unveil a new [[Domedag]] exhibit -- even though it's been decades since 0 AP, researchers are still finding more information from the corpse and abyss
		- The new main display is rumored to be part of Domedag's corpse, which has never rotted
		- Rumors are that a claw or tooth may be shown, which is very rare -- the [[Domedag Researchers]] rarely allow any specimens outside [[Domedag's Abyss City]]
		- Other rumors claim that it may be information on what lies within [[Domedag's Abyss]] -- only a few explorers have returned to the surface, as magical teleportation is blocked
- [[The Siphon Map]] is hidden within a permanent display in [[The Museum]]: the [[Common Rosetta Stone]]

### Encounters
- [[The Gala Event]] begins, and the PCs notice things going wrong
	- One of [[The Naturalists]] has knocked out a guard and assumed their identity
	- A handful of the "guests" are part of [[The Naturalists]] and are gathering around various exhibits to get into position
		- One of them mentions the cue they're waiting for
	- Some of the seals around the taxidermized displays have been altered/destroyed subtly
- There is a major event intended to happen as the 3 comets pass overhead
	- *Maybe there's a planetarium with a domed glass roof set up to light up as the comets converge -- and this happens during cocktail hour*
- [[The Naturalists]] move off a cue of the 3 comets passing overhead and take action
	- They believe that [[The Siphon Map]] is hidden in [[Common Rosetta Stone]], and a few of them dash towards the basement doors
		- The undercover guard lets them through and watches the door -- this is clearly a coordinated effort
	- The undercover guests spring into action and use necromancy to reanimate the taxidermized exhibits and cause chaos for the guards to deal with
		- The rich guests have some personal bodyguards
	- The museum goes into lockdown, and the walls prevent teleportation
- The PCs follow the infiltrators into the basement
	- The infiltrators notably rush past rooms with expensive artifacts (e.g. gold, diamonds) and beeline for where [[Common Rosetta Stone]] is being stored
	- As the PCs and infiltrators arrive, they see [[The Map Room]] glow -- the 3 comets converge and the map is projected
		- One of the infiltrators takes out a prepared parchment and quill and starts copying down the map
		- The other infiltrators attempt to keep the party away from that one, and help them flee
		- The infiltrators will try to take [[Common Rosetta Stone]] with them
# Act 2: Search for the Siphon
## Goals
- All 3 Key Trials have been unlocked and the keys are out in the world
- Learn about [[The Equalists]]' goals
	- Learn that they are willing to go to dire lengths to get their message out -- e.g. bombing schools
	- Learn that they are interpreting "sacred texts" that Nackan wrote for their own purposes
- Learn more about [[The Order of the Golden Scales]]' goals -- specifically, that they are trying to destroy [[The Siphon]]
	- Learn that [[The Order of the Golden Scales]] suspects they can use [[Domedag's Abyss]] to destroy [[The Siphon]]
	- Visit [[Domedag's Abyss City]] and learn about the history of [[Domedag]] and [[Domedag's Abyss]]
- Learn more about [[The Naturalists]]' goals
	- Learn that [[The Naturalists]]' leader plans to reanimate [[Domedag]] and reconquer Kyrka in the name of [[The Ekedalia Family]] -- because the leader is actually [[Last Ekedalian King]]!

**Optional Goals**
- Find a replacement for [[The Naturalists]]' leader on [[The Board]]
	- Maybe ally with [[Diviner Board Member]] and attempt to reform Pinntorp's system?
- Work against [[The Equalists]]' message of extreme bigotry against magic
	- Help bridge [[Nackanas]] and [[Pinntorp]] by supporting [[The Artificers Guild]]

# Act 3: Race for the Siphon
##  Goals
- Prevent [[The Naturalists]] from using [[The Siphon]] to reanimated [[Domedag]]
- Prevent [[The Equalists]] from using [[The Siphon]] to remove magic from all Kyrkans
	- Maybe [[The Equalists]] are going to try and use a magnifying device from [[The Artificers Guild]], and then destroy the guild afterwards
		- OR blame it on the guild, to turn the people away from any sort of scientific-arcane partnership in the future
- Determine what to do with [[The Order of the Golden Scales]]
	- They could help them try to destroy [[The Siphon]] in [[Domedag's Abyss]], or use it for their own purposes
