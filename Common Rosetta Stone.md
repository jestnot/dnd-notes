This is a large slab of stone dating back to the [[Age of Origins]] and contains writing in ancient Draconic, ancient Dwarvish, ancient Elvish, and ancient Gnomish. Many believe this historical relic was used by ancient Kyrkans to develop the language of Common.

This is a permanent display in [[The Museum]].
