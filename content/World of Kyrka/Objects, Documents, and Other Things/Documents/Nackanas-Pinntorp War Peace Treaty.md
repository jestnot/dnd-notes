This treaty was written in 191 AP by [[The Board]] and [[last Nackanian Prime Minister]] to end the [[Nackanas-Pinntorp War]].
# Notable Terms
- The borders of [[Nackanas]] and [[Pinntorp]] were officially changed so that [[Area that triggered N-P War]] was considered Pinntorper territory.
- Pinntorper mages entered Nackanian territories to heal [[illness]] from affected Nackanians (under the watchful eye of Nackanian guards).
- Nackanas and Pinntorp would have any national contracts brokered with [[Vangsta]] for stones or metals signed off by the other nation.
	- As this term was set by the Nackanian and Pinntorper governments with each other, [[Gamvaluta]] had no say in this, but was notably frustrated by the additional red tape that prolonged contract creation.
