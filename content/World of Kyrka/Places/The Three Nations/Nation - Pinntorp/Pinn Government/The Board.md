The Board is one of the governing bodies of [[Pinntorp]].

Within the Board, there are two unofficial parties that have formed over time, with the ninth member historically acting as a tie-breaker
- Abjuration, Enchantment, Evocation, and Necromancy
- Conjuration, Divination, Illusion, Transmutation
