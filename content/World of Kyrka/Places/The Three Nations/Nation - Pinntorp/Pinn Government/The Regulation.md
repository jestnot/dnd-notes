The Regulation is one of the governing bodies of [[Pinntorp]].

It is a group of officials elected for their prowess in calculation -- they are responsible for conducting national votes and calculating how many votes each Pinntorper citizen gets.

Each Pinntorper's number of votes is updated annually based on a complicated (and public) calculation to determine how much that Pinntorper has contributed to society, with a heavy emphasis on academic contribution to the arcane arts.
