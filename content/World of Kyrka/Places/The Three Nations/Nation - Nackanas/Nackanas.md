> ...and so we stand here today, 
*Inauguration speech of Hampus Dalin, first Prime Minister of Nackanas*

Citizens are referred to as "Nackanians" or "Nacks."
## Government
- Governing body: Prime Minister ([[Nackanian Prime Minister]]), [[The Cabinet]], and [[The Council]].
- Each Nackanian gets one vote for their regional Council member.
- Each government official has a fixed term of no more than a few years.
## Known For
- Advancing non-arcane forms of technology and science.
- Being named after [[Nackan Qvist]], whom most Nackanians refer to as "the Lady of the People."
	- Orphans born and/or raised in Nackanas tend to adopt the last name "Qvist."
- [[Nackan's Peak]], the famous cliff where [[Nackan Qvist]] and the Union (her squadron) jumped off to enter Domedag's maw and deliver the killing blow. This is a site of pilgrimage for those who believe in Nackan's ideals.
