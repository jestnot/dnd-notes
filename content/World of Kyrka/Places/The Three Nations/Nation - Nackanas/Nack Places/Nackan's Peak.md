Nackan's Peak is the famous cliff where [[Nackan Qvist]] and the Union (her squadron) jumped off to enter [[Domedag]]'s maw and deliver the killing blow. This is a site of pilgrimage for those who believe in Nackan's ideals.

It is located in the southwestern part of [[Nackanas]] and is adjacent to [[Domedag's Abyss]].
