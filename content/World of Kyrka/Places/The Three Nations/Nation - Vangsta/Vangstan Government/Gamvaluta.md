 Pronounced "GAHM-vah-LOO-tah."

Gamvaluta (meaning "Old Money" in dwarvish) governs [[Vangsta]] and holds absolute power over the mines in the [[Vesken Mountains]]. They are currently the strongest players in Kyrka's economy.
# Beliefs
- "Money is power."
	- The nation's priority is maintaining Vangsta's strong economic power.
	- Gamvaluta was notably not a part of the [[Nackanas-Pinntorp War Peace Treaty]] and were frustrated by the added conditions to brokering national contracts with [[Nackanas]] and [[Pinntorp]]. Some members have publicly referred to the treaty as "a bureaucratic nightmare."
- "Dwarves know the stones best."
	- Most of the [[Vesken Mountains]] (specifically, [[Ivar]] and the mines) are only open to dwarves.
