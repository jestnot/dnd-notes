Pronounced "NAI-vah-LOO-tah."

Nyvaluta (meaning "New Money" in dwarvish) is a relatively new grassroots organization that publicly disagrees with many of [[Gamvaluta]]'s policies.

They were officially formed in 189 AP, by outraged Vangstans who demanded [[Gamvaluta]] stop profiting off both sides of the [[Nackanas-Pinntorp War]] and use its stance as a third party to end the war.

Since the war ended, they have maintained an unofficial presence in [[Vinterfint]].
# Beliefs
- "Vangsta should use its economic power to help all Vangstans"
	- Money (and power) should not be hoarded exclusively to the denizens of the [[Vesken Mountains]]; Vangsta is a much wider nation.
	- [[Gamvaluta]]'s insistence on dwarven superiority is too similar to how [[The Ekedalia Family]] hoarded knowledge of the arcane arts during the [[Age of Stagnancy (or Stability)]].
