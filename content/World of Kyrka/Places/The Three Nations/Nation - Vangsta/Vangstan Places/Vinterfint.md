The largest trade city in Kyrka, and a prominent city in [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/The Three Nations/Nation - Vangsta/Vangsta]].

Due to Gamvaluta's neutral stance, many Nackanian and Pinntorper merchants trade in Vinterfint, rather than crossing the Nackanas-Pinntorp border. This has led to the rise of rich Vangstan merchants acting as middlemen in Vinterfint.
