Citizens are referred to as "Vangstans."
# Government
- [[Gamvaluta]] ("GAHM-vah-LOO-tah," meaning "Old Money" in dwarvish) governs Vangsta and holds absolute power over the mines in the Vesken Mountains; their economic power has been unparallelled throughout Kyrka's recorded history
- [[Nyvaluta]] ("NAI-vah-LOO-tah," meaning "New Money" in dwarvish) is a group who publicly disagrees with Gamvaluta's policies
# Known For
- The precious stones and metals that are only sourced from deep within the [[Vesken Mountains]]
	- Nearly all of these gems have only been found here, and are key material components in much of spellcasting (e.g. diamonds) as well as in new scientific studies (e.g. the new area of "chemistry")
	- While some metals and stones can be found elsewhere in Kyrka, those sources are typically outside of civilized borders and infested with monsters and are not nearly as plentiful as the Vesken Mountains
- [[Vinterfint]], the largest trade city in Kyrka
