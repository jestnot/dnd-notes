Citizens are referred to as "Nackanians" or "Nacks."

## Government
- Prime Minister (([[Nackanian Prime Minister]]), [[The Cabinet]], and [[The Council]]
- Each Nackanian gets one vote for their regional Council member
- Each government official has a fixed term of no more than a few years

## Known For
- Advancing non-arcane forms of technology and science
- The remaining major presence of [[The Church]], in a new Nackanian branch that focuses on emulating the ideology of Nackan Qvist, the savior of Kyrka
- Recent experimentation with "black powder" from deep within the ground, where Domedag burrowed to the surface
- [[Nackan's Peak]], the famous cliff where [[Nackan Qvist]] and her team battled Domedag and were swallowed whole
	- This is a site of pilgrimage for those who believe in Nackan's ideals (the foundation of modern thought)
