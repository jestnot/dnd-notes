Pinntorp has no central governing body; all decisions are made through citizen voting. The Regulation is a group of officials elected for their prowess in calculation—they are responsible for conducting national votes and calculating how many votes each Pinntorper citizen gets. Each Pinntorper's number of votes is updated annually based on a complicated (and public) calculation to determine how much that Pinntorper has contributed to society, with a heavy emphasis on academic contribution to the arcane arts.
