Citizens are referred to as "Pinntorpers" or "Pinns."

# Government
- Each citizen is granted a number of votes based a complicated calculation of their contribution to society, with a heavy focus on their contribution to their academic contribution to the arcane arts
- [[The Regulation]] is in charge of recalculating each citizen's votes annually, and running all votes for national decisions
- [[The Board]] is an emergency group of high-ranking scholars who can temporarily take full control of Pinntorp in case of a world-threatening emergency
	- The Board consists of the most prominent scholar in each of the eight schools of magic, as well as a ninth member who is considered the most well-rounded scholar across all schools of magic

# Known For
- Arcane studies (including arcane technology), including new schools and libraries which are open to all peoples
- The greatest advancements in the arcane arts; it is common for many citizens to know at least a cantrip, and for even the smallest towns to use common magic items to improve their everyday life
