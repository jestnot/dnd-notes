The Kingdom of Ekedalen no longer exists as of the beginning of the [[content/History of Kyrka/Ages/Age of Progress]].

# Government
The government consisted of a feudal system.

- **Monarch:** The head of the Ekedalia family and ruler of the Ekedalian kingdom
- **Nobles:** The monarch chose the head of each noble family, and these were only comprised of the longest-lifespan races (specifically, elves, gnomes, and dwarves)
	- Each noble family was appointed a demesne by the monarch, with the more favored families closer to Ekedalen
	- The monarch reserved the right to revoke power at any time, and was strict on keeping noble families pure to their race (i.e. having a half-elf child was fair ground for the entire family losing their nobility status)
	- The head of each family was an inherited position
- **Knights & Druids:** Each noble appointed a Knight (head of security) and Druid (head of agriculture) to protect and support their land
	- These positions were not necessarily inherited, although many were for generations
	- These positions did not need to strictly be given to elves/gnomes/dwarves, but they often were given to a person with a longer lifespan
- **Serfs:** The remainder (and majority) of the population

# Known For
- [[The Church]]'s strong connection and support of [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/Ekedalen (no longer existent)/Ekedalian Government/The Ekedalia Family]].
- A strong emphasis on prioritizing long-lifespan races over others—the arcane arts were exclusively taught to the royal family and nobles
