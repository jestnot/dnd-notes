Nackan was a female human.

Once a human serf, Nackan Qvist is one of the most prominent figures in recent history. She is generally known as the person who rallied Kyrkans after the Kingdom of [[Ekedalen]]'s destruction, and led the charge to defeat [[Domedag]].

Before heading into her final battle, she provided guidance to the other main Kyrkans who rose to power on how she hoped the new world would be. Many believe that she was a strong influence on [[Three Nation Treaty]].
# The Union
Her squadron of warriors were known as "the Union." In their final battle, they famously leaped off a cliff (later known as [[Nackan's Peak]]) in a coordinated move to enter [[Domedag]]'s maw and strike the killing blow from the inside.
# Influence on Nackanas
Sometimes referred to as "**the Lady of the People**" by Nackanians, the nation of [[Nackanas]] was named after her and its policies were set up to follow her core beliefs.
