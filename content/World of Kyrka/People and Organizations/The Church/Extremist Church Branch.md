This branch of the church split off due to their strong belief that [[The Three Sisters]]' most recent test for mortals has still not yet been passed. Most of the Church believes that it *was* passed successfully when [[Nackan Qvist]] rallied humanoids to kill [[Domedag]]; they do not support this branch's beliefs. To this last point, they are especially unwelcome in [[Nackanas]].

Most followers currently reside in [[Extremist Church Branch Village]].
# Beliefs
They believe that the most recent test was not for mortals to destroy [[Domedag]], but to fundamentally prove humanoids worthy of being the prevailing ruling mortal class in Kyrka.

They claim that their belief is supported because:
- [[Domedag]]'s corpse has never rotted, which could indicate that the test is not yet over
- [[The Three Sisters]] have remained silent even after [[Domedag]]'s defeat -- surely, they would reach out and confirm that the mortals have passed if they did
	- The Sisters continued to communicate with mortals after the first two tests were passed, so it follows that they should return once more
- The past two tests ended with a new mortal ruling class taking over (the giants took over from the dragons, and the humanoids took over from the giants)
	- Hence, the next clear step is for humanoids to prove their worth and solidify their place in the hierarchy
	- (The only other option would be to surrender power to another class of mortals, which certainly could not be the answer)

They believe that humanoids can pass this test by proving to [[The Three Sisters]] that all Kyrkan humanoids have accepted their role as mortals in the hierarchy with gods.
- That is, no humanoid should have the hubris to believe they are capable of godly actions and power
- This branch practices a humble lifestyle to prove their diligence to this belief daily
- This branch also aims to convince all humanoids of this belief, and has been rumored to punish those whom they believe fall prey to this sin
