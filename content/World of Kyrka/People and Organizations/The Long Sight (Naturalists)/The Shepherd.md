## Description
- Is actually [[Makkalos Ekedalia]]
- Leader of the Cult of the Long Sight
- Promises the cult a future "paradise" for Kyrka, where long-lifespan races reign again, and short-lifespan races support them
	- A return to the way of life gods intended -- if the gods didn't want this, then why make some races live longer than others? (i.e. be naturally superior)
	- [[Last Ekedalian King]] failed the gods' test by focusing on just Ekedalen at the end; that is why Domedag targeted it
	- The Shepherd will be different -- all people will be grateful for his rule, especially when they see how the Shepherd can protect them and provide for them
