- Following the dragons’ downfall, giants battled other creatures to fill the power vacuum. Giant tribes were largely nomadic, but kept to certain regions to draw power from the natural climates.
- This age came to an end when giants were overrun by an influx of monstrosities on the continent, leaving humanoid races to take control and build civilizations.
	- [[The Church]] believed this was [[The Three Sisters]]’ second test for mortals.
	- While giants still roam the world, they largely avoid humanoid civilizations. Some say that the giants blame humanoids for not joining in the fight, despite the giants teaching some arcane and druidic knowledge. Others believe that the giants avoid civilization out of a deep shame that they were not able to protect their own people.
