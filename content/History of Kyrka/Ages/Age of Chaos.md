- With powerful creatures vying for power after the giant tribes were largely wiped out, various civilizations were attempted to be created, only to be destroyed by neighboring enemies.
	- This age was marked by constant warfare on smaller scales. While there were some larger civilizations that sustained themselves for a few decades, monstrosities and other creatures were a prevalent danger.
- Based on public record from the Age of Stagnancy (then referred to as the "[[Age of Stability]]"), this age came to an end when [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/Ekedalen (no longer existent)/Ekedalian Government/First Ekedalian King]] received a divine blessing from [[The Three Sisters]] and wielded power to establish a warded and flourishing civilization: the Kingdom of [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/Ekedalen (no longer existent)/Ekedalen]].
- Only referred to in name (chosen by [[content/World of Kyrka/Places/Ekedalen (no longer existent)/Ekedalian Government/The Ekedalia Family]]) after the age came to an end.
