This war was the culmination of recent tensions between [[Nackanas]] and [[Pinntorp]] and was triggered by a land dispute over [[Area that triggered N-P War]]. It took place **from 189 AP to 191 AP**.
# Events
- **188 AP**
	- Nackanas and [[Vangsta]] struck the largest deal in the [[Age of Progress]] to date: an exclusive contract for Vangsta to ship certain stones and metals to Nackanas for scientific research, in return for a vast sum of money (higher than the rate set by [[Gamvaluta]] at the time).
- **189 AP**
	- Pinntorp claimed that there was an area of land in Nackanian territory that should have gone to Pinntorp based on details in the [[Three Nation Treaty]].
	- Nackanas disagreed, claiming that Pinntorp was inventing an excuse so they could expand their land and convert Nackanians to their "blasphemous" views on the arcane arts.
	- [[The Board]] and the Nackanian Prime Minister of the time ([[last Nackanian Prime Minister]]) met to discuss the dispute and to look at the original copy of the [[Three Nation Treaty]] together. They spent three weeks in discussions before they were interrupted.
		- In [[Area that triggered N-P War]], an unknown illness (later known as [[illness]])broke out. Nackanians were unable to determine a treatment, so nearby Pinntorpers in [[pinn area for war]] (just on the other side of the border from [[Area that triggered N-P War]] offered to use healing magic.
		- [[last Nackanian Prime Minister]] claimed that Pinntorp had covertly caused the illness and were trying to sway the local Nackanians to join [[pinn area for war]], instead of going through the proper diplomatic channels. [[last Nackanian Prime Minister]] sent the Nackanian border guard to protect Nackanians.
	- A violent encounter between the guard and local Pinntorpers resulted in the death of a Pinntorper, ceasing political discussions.
		- [[The Board]] sent the Pinntorper border guard to defend Pinntorpers in [[pinn area for war]].
		- As tensions rose, another violent encounter (this time between the guards of both nations) escalated into the first battle.
- **189-191 AP** (during the war)
	- During the war, [[Vangsta]] claimed to maintain a neutral stance by balancing any new contracts it created with Nackanas and Pinntorp to support war efforts.
		- A group of Vangstans who disagreed with [[Gamvaluta]]'s decision to profit off the war began publicly vocalizing their critiques. They named themselves [[Nyvaluta]] and organized protests to demand that Vangsta use its position as a third party to champion an end to their war.
	- [[illness]] continued to spread past [[Area that triggered N-P War]] and affected Nackanians and Pinntorpers alike.
		- Pinntorp dealt with the illness relatively easily; the Board called for necromancers specializing in healing to be dispatched to the affected areas, and to maintain a presence near the border to prevent further outbreak.
		- Nackanas was heavily affected. Towards the end **189 AP**, [[last Nackanian Prime Minister]] arranged for top scientists put their projects on hold and help research a cure with government funding. This move had strong national support, and many believe it was crucial in [[last Nackanian Prime Minister]] securing votes for **their?** second term.
- **191 AP**
	- By **191 AP**, Nackanian scientists were only able to devise a treatment that mitigated [[illness]] symptoms, but not a permanent cure. [[illness]] continued to spread west and affect [[big nackanian city]], so [[last Nackanian Prime Minister]] called for negotiations with [[The Board]].
	- After two days of discussion, both parties signed the [[Nackanas-Pinntorp War Peace Treaty]] and officially declared the war to be over.
